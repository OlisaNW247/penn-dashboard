# Privacy Policy — Low Hanging Fruit (LHF)

_Last updated: 2026-06-07_

Low Hanging Fruit ("LHF", "the app") is a personal academic dashboard that helps
you see your upcoming Canvas assignments and deadlines in one place. This policy
explains what the app does with your information. In short: **everything stays on
your device, and we collect nothing.**

## What the app accesses
- **Canvas login.** You sign in to your own Canvas account through Canvas's own
  web page, shown inside the app. The app never sees or stores your password.
- **Canvas calendar feed.** After you log in, the app captures your personal
  Canvas calendar feed URL and reads your assignments/deadlines from it.
- **Assignments you create.** Any one-off or recurring tasks you add yourself.

## Where your data lives
- **On your device only.** Your Canvas session, calendar-feed URL, the
  assignments shown, your completed/edited items, and any tasks you create are
  stored locally on your device.
- **No servers of ours.** The app has no backend. It talks only directly to
  Canvas (`canvas.upenn.edu`) to fetch your assignments. We operate no server and
  never receive your data.

## What we collect and share
- **Nothing.** We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. There
  is no analytics, tracking, or advertising in the app.

## Notifications
- If you enable reminders, they are scheduled and delivered **locally** on your
  device. No notification data leaves the device.

## Your control
- You can disconnect or stop using the app at any time.
- **Deleting the app removes all of its data** from your device.

## Children's privacy
The app is intended for university students and is not directed at children under 13.
